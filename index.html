🏠 Am I at risk? Address flood check
Guidance only, not a flood determination. Life-threatening emergency: call 911. This reads live NWS/NOAA/USGS data near a point; it never says you are safe. A missing nearby gauge or alert does not mean no risk; verify locally. To place the pin, your typed address is sent to the OpenStreetMap geocoder to convert it to coordinates; it is not stored, logged, or shared beyond that one lookup.
